### Maya’s first stop: empathy and guidance from Ebb

Maya is feeling overwhelmed by work, caregiving, and sleep trouble. She opens the Headspace app and connects with Ebb, the AI companion. Ebb listens patiently and responds with empathy, helping Maya feel seen as she describes what she’s going through. Ebb then guides her through a calming breathing exercise, followed by a short clinical assessment.

### Maya is matched with a licensed therapist in less than 1 day

Based on her preferences, Maya is matched with a Headspace therapist who specializes in anxiety and stress. Together, they work on navigating the challenges of balancing work with life at home as a caregiver.

### Specialized support for complex needs

Based on Maya’s ongoing assessments and therapy sessions, her therapist recognizes that she could potentially benefit from medication to support her progress. A psychiatrist is added to her care team for medication management and specialized treatment.

### From recovery to resilience with Headspace coaching

Maya starts working with a Headspace mental health coach to maintain her progress and build resilience. Headspace coaches and clinicians use Headspace's connected care system, so her coach picks up right where therapy left off. This seamless handoff helps members stay on track, and reduces the likelihood of returning to high-cost clinical care.

### Everyday tools to build resilience

Maya has access to over 5,000 award-winning exercises, including meditations, guided programs, and sleep support — so she can proactively care for her mental health. These personalized tools help her stay productive and engaged at work, build resilience, and grow as a leader.

### Support for everyday factors that affect mental health

Headspace helps Maya navigate life’s everyday stressors, like financial planning and childcare. Practical tools and referrals ease daily burdens and free up mental energy.

### Maya still has a full plate, but now she also has full support - so she can show up to work as her best self.

From Ebb to therapy to work-life services, Headspace helps Maya find relief, build resilience, and get the help she needs every step of the way. With ongoing support, she’s better equipped to handle challenges — and show up to work feeling and doing her best.
