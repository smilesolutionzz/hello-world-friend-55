### Wellcome Trust awards £5.3 miillion grant to Wysa    [**Funding**](https://blogs.wysa.io/blog/company-news/wellcome-awards-5-3m-grant-to-wysa-3)[**for digital mental health intervention for adolescent girls in India**](https://blogs.wysa.io/blog/company-news/wellcome-awards-5-3m-grant-to-wysa-3)

### Wysa cited in Singapore Parliament for mental health support    [**Named in parliamentary debate on digital mental health support for young people**](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7436720650813190144/)





















Senior Minister of State for Health Dr Poh-Koon Koh cited Wysa in Singapore's Parliament while addressing questions on mental health support for teenagers and young adults. Wysa powers mindline.sg, one of Singapore's first digital mental health platforms, providing 24/7 CBT-based support to supplement professional services.

### AI-powered support nearly triples coaching session completion    [**Peer-reviewed study published in JMIR finds significant uplift in engagement**](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7432409059665850368/)





















A peer-reviewed study of 1,213 Wysa users found that access to AI-powered support between coaching sessions makes users nearly three times more likely to complete their coaching programme. Users with app access averaged 6.57 sessions, compared to 0.55 for those using coaching alone.

### Wysa launches Dreamkit to expand mental health access in rural India    [**A phygital approach to building emotional resilience in low-resource settings**](https://wysa.aflip.in/wiseupissue08\#page/20)





















Wysa has launched Dreamkit in Maharashtra, combining physical workbooks and clinically validated digital tools to bring mental health support to adolescent girls in rural communities where access to care is limited. Co-designed with local partners and delivered in Marathi, the programme bridges the gap between technology and the people who need it most.
